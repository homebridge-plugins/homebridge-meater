homebridge-meater

The Homebridge Meater plugin allows you to access your Meater Device(s) from HomeKit with Homebridge.

Installation

  1. Search for "Meater" on the plugin screen of Homebridge UI
  2. Find: homebridge-meater
  3. Click Install

Configuration

  1. Download Meater App on App Store or Google Play Store
  2. Register a meater account and log in into your account
  3. Restart Homebridge/Child Bridge
